ENAS andras o opoios kratouse mia bala sto xeri kai epaize me aftin xtipontas tin sto patoma perpatouse me tin gunaika tou
i opoia esproxne to paidi tous se ena karotsi  sto dromo. Apenadi tous (stin mesi briskotan enas mikros autokinitodromos) 
ebgaze mia gunaika i opoia eixe k ena skilaki ta psonia tis apo to port bagaz. Otan to zevgari stekotan stin akri tou dromou
kai ithele na ton perasei, i bala tou xefuge kai kilise pros tin meria tis gunaikas me to skuli diladi ston autokinitodromo. 
To skuli amesos ertexe ston dromo gia na piasei tin bala. Apo tin tarachi, epesan tis gunaikas ta psonia kato sto dromo.Tin 
idia stigmi p to skulaki ertexe stin mesi tou dromou gia na piasei tin bala, plisiaze ena amaxi to opoio ithele na perasei  
alla anagastike ta frenarei apotoma gia na min patisei kanenan. Etsi to amaxi pou briskotan piso apo ekeino to xtipise apo piso gt
den prolabe na frenarei engeros. Akoustike enas dinatos thoribos kai etsi katalaba oti eixe ginei zimia sta amaxia. O pateras 
pou kratouse prin tin bala boithise tin gunaika tis opoias eixan paisei ta psonia apo tin sakoula na ta xanamazepsei. 
Tin idia wra oi duo odigoi kalesan tin germanini astinomia me to kinito tous gia ta katagrapsei to atuxima.
